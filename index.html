Dad Truths

Practical dad advice is hard to find

NCT classes are great, and there is no shortage of books on parenting, but finding recommendations on what to buy, what to try when things aren't working, and what to expect day to day before you have children are thin on the ground.

Being a dad today is very different to thirty years ago

Whereas being a mother has remained relatively unchanged, it is likely your own father had a very different experience in bringing up children to the one you will have. This means finding someone to discuss the challenges with can sometimes be difficult, especially if your friends haven't started having kids yet.

Being a dad can sometimes be a lonely experience

If your partner chooses to breastfeed, in the early days of fatherhood you may find yourself relegated to the nappy-changing-house-tidying-chef. That's not to say it's not a key household position, but it's a very different relationship to the keeping-the-baby-alive-by-feeding-it-with-my-body one that your partner will likely have.
