The first time out alone with your baby is a great and terrifying experience. There are some dad truths that you need to accept when being out and about:

* At some point your baby will do a poo that will cover their entire body, and most likely you. This will probably happen in a crowded cafe or on public transport
* At least once you will push a screaming baby around a supermarket
* You will leave the house without your changing bag, and only realise when the inevitable smell reaches your nose
* Having ordered a coffee, you will go to change a nappy only to find out the changing tables are located in the women's toilets
  
## Marginal Gains do not Apply

You do not cycle for team Sky. Do not worry about saving weight. If you think you will need something one time out of ten, then pack it. Start off cautious and then learn over time what you can leave out. A lot of this is obviously age dependent. For example: plasters. Not necessary for babies, and rarely necessary for toddlers, but when you need them, you need them. Plus they are tiny. Conclusion: pack 'em.

## The Essentials

So you've got yourself a nice [changing bag](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B07G1S17KG/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1634&creative=6738&creativeASIN=B07G1S17KG&linkCode=as2&tag=howtodad-21&linkId=8c40fabda8e6784dca5d2db1b018a68f). What should you put in it?

* **Nappies**. Plan for one an hour when young, plus a few extra for those emergencies.

* **Wipes**. For obvious reasons.

* [**Mobile changing mat**](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B0793SPCBX/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1634&creative=6738&creativeASIN=B0793SPCBX&linkCode=as2&tag=howtodad-21&linkId=0cd5f3a49b97bb86e73d497251550de8). Changing tables in supermarkets and cafes are often made of hard plastic, so putting this down first will make it much more comfortable.

* **Alcohol hand gel or**[**disinfectant wipes**](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B006H4R4D4/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1634&creative=6738&creativeASIN=B006H4R4D4&linkCode=as2&tag=howtodad-21&linkId=38252d87dee5e2751ed9dcdb3ccdad6e). Useful for wiping your own hands, or other surfaces that might be dirty before or after use.

* **Nappy bags**. They aren't useful just for nappies. Dirty clothes or toys can go in them too.

* **A change of clothes**. This should really be number one. You will need these.
